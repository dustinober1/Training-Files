Four fun facts about Triceratops

  • Fact #3: The triceratops can lived in the Cretaceous Period.
  • Fact #4: The triceratops only lived in modern day North America.

Triceratops was a herbivore that ate shurbs and plants. It used its horns to know over small trees to get to food. Triceratops had 400 to 800 teeth that were replaced throughout their lifetime.
